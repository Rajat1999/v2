At TOI’s roundtable on Delhi’s traffic and congestion problem last month, the government was not in favour of constructing more parking lots. People buy a car for Rs 2 lakh and expect the government to construct parking for it for Rs 10 lakh. Why should the government do that? The government is not responsible for creating more parking for people. Parking is the responsibility of the person who has bought the car. While the government is firm on changing the parking policy, officials say it will take time to implement it. “The government will have to strengthen the public transport system before increasing parking rates. It is a long-term plan. We can’t inconvenience people by increasing the parking rates without providing adequate public transport,” said a source in the government.
